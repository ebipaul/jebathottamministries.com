About Our Church

Rooted in faith. Growing in grace. Reaching the world.

Since 1999

Our History

Grace Community Church was founded in 1999 by Rev. Emmanuel Osei and a small group of twelve believers meeting in a living room in Chennai. From humble beginnings, God's faithfulness has grown us into a congregation of over 4,000 members spanning 18 nations.

Through two decades of ministry, we have planted 14 daughter churches, built schools, established a food bank serving 500 families monthly, and trained hundreds of leaders through our Grace Bible Institute.

Our story is not one of a great church, but of a great God working through ordinary people who dared to believe.

Why We Exist

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God passionately, serve people sacrificially, and transform communities powerfully — starting in Chennai and reaching to the ends of the earth.

Our Vision

A church where every person finds family, every seeker discovers truth, every believer is equipped for their calling — and every city is touched by the love and justice of the Kingdom of God.

Scripture

We believe the Bible is God's inspired Word, our ultimate authority in all matters of faith and life.

Community

We are better together. Biblical community is where transformation happens, where we bear one another's burdens.

Mission

Every believer is called. We equip and send disciples to every sphere of society and every corner of the globe.
